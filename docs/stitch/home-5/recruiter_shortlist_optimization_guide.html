# Recruiter Shortlist Optimization: AI/ML Engineer Profile To move your profile from "Good" to "100% Recruiter Shortlist," we need to shift the focus from *what you did* to *the impact and scalability* of your work. Recruiters for high-level AI/ML roles look for "Production-Grade" signals. ## 1. The "10-Second" Rule (Hero Section) Recruiters spend 10 seconds on your site before deciding to read more. * **Current:** "Building machine learning systems..." (Action-oriented) * **Optimization:** "Deploying scalable ML systems and NLP pipelines that drive business value." (Outcome-oriented) * **Key Signal:** Mention "Production" and "Scale" immediately. ## 2. GitHub Professionalism (The Repo) * **Commit History:** Ensure your commit messages are professional (e.g., "feat: implement RAG with FAISS" instead of "update code"). * **Code Quality:** Recruiters often click into the `script.js` or `index.html`. Ensure the code is clean, commented, and follows DRY (Don't Repeat Yourself) principles. * **Metadata:** (Already suggested) Ensure the repo description and tags are filled out to help with GitHub's internal search. ## 3. Project Evidence (The "Proof") * **System Architecture Diagrams:** For your 3 main projects, a simple visual diagram of the data flow (Data -> Model -> Inference -> UI) is a massive green flag. It shows you understand the *system*, not just the *model*. * **Quantified Outcomes:** "98.4% accuracy" is good, but "Reduced false positives by 15% compared to baseline" is better. It shows you know how to measure success. ## 4. Technical Breadth vs. Depth * **Breadth:** Keep the 6 capability cards (ML, NLP, RAG, etc.). * **Depth:** In the experience section, name-drop the specific versions or libraries (e.g., "PyTorch 2.x", "AWS SageMaker", "LangGraph"). ## 5. Contact & Accessibility * **Floating CTA:** On mobile, having a small, persistent "Download Resume" or "Contact" button at the bottom can increase conversion. --- ### Implementation Plan: 1. **Regenerate Hero:** Focus on "Production-Ready" and "Business Value." 2. **Enhance Project Cards:** Add a "Key Impact" line to each. 3. **Add Architecture Cues:** Use subtle background patterns or icons that hint at system design. 4. **Refine Navigation:** Ensure the resume is always one click away.
